Robot Shooter

Robot Shooter is a fast-paced defense shooter where the player must stop waves of robots attempting to cross a multi-floor building. Positioned on a distant platform, the player must eliminate the robots before they reach the end of the third floor.

Robots spawn on the first floor and navigate through the building toward their objective. As they move between floors, they may temporarily hide behind obstacles for protection, forcing the player to react quickly and aim carefully.

Obstacles can be destroyed by repeatedly shooting them, allowing you to expose the robots and continue your defense. However, these obstacles will regenerate after some time, adding an extra layer of strategy and timing to the gameplay.

Your goal is simple: shoot down as many robots as possible and earn points while preventing them from advancing through the building.

Key Features

Multi-Floor Enemy Navigation – Robots spawn on the first floor and attempt to reach the end of the third floor.

Obstacle System – Enemies can hide behind destructible cover.

Destructible Environment – Obstacles can be destroyed with repeated shots.

Regenerating Cover – Destroyed obstacles reappear after some time.

Score System – Earn points for every robot you eliminate.

Defensive Shooter Gameplay – Stop enemies before they complete their path.
